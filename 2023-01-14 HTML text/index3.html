Story for motivation

Introduction

Ahmadi Mtizi is a 100-year-old maize farmer

from Tanzania, and thanks to donors like you, he can see again.

Maize farming is a tough job, regardless of age. Ahmadi, still strong at 100 years,

took pride in caring for his family. However, when he began to

lose his vision he also lost his ability to provide for his family .

Ahmadi soon learned of Seva’s local vision screening camp,

where he was diagnosed with a cataract. Thanks to a local Seva partner clinic,

he happily returned the next day for sight-restoring surgery at no cost to him.

After the surgery, when the doctors removed his eyepatch, Ahmadi was spellbound.

“I am very happy. Very happy,” he said in English.

Today he tends to his crops, provides for his family,

and continues to live independently and with dignity thanks to your support.

The End
